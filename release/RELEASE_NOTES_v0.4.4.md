# StatsQR v0.4.4

## Changed
- Reduced the automatic sharing timeout from 10 minutes to 2 minutes
- Added a Wi‑Fi confirmation prompt when starting sharing with Wi‑Fi turned off
- After confirmation, StatsQR asks KOReader to bring Wi‑Fi online and retries automatically when networking becomes ready

## Package
- Includes the `statsqr.koplugin` folder ready to copy to your KOReader plugins directory
